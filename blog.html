What are some efficient alternatives of gas stove for cooking?

Answered on 19th Jul 2020.

No second thoughts. Induction Cooker is the most efficient alternative to the Gas stove for cooking.

I am giving you the advantages below. Due to these advantages, it is becoming widely popular among the masses. Besides many applications, a special mention to people living on rent, students and working executives who live away from their families in PGs at various locations, this is the ideal product for them to carry around and cooking.

There are a great many advantages for using an Induction cooker for cooking:

  1. First of all, it is very compact and goes well aesthetically with your Kitchen. you can also carry it to the convenient place to another room.
  2. It is more energy-efficient. There is low heat loss while cooking since heat is transferred through the principle of induction.
  3. It is a cool surface cooktop. The surface of the cooktop is never too hot since there is no direct flame to get it hot. It only heats up the pan and not the surrounding.
  4. It is very easy to clean. Think of cleaning the Gas stove in case of milk boiling and spilling on it.
  5. It cooks faster and saves time. It cooks almost 44% faster than the Gas stove.
  6. It is easy to use. It has presets that are programmed as per the dishes, so no worrying about the sim and adjusting temperatures.
  7. It is easy to maintain and repair. There are no hassles of cockroaches entering the cooktop and clogging the tubes.
  8. Last, but the least -the cooking using Induction Stove Power is far cheaper than on the Gas stove.
